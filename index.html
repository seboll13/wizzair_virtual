WHO ARE WE ?

A little description of our virtual airline

We are a new virtual airline association looking to replicate most Wizzair flights for virtual pilots to make their experience as realistic as possible in Infinite Flight.

On this website, you will have the possibility to learn who we are and what we do, look at the type of flights we do, enjoy some nice pictures and contact us if you have any questions or simply want to be part of our association.

Please note that Wizz Air Virtual is a non-profit organization officially approved and acknowledged by the Infinite Flight Virtual Airline Regulatory Board. All logos, brand names, and pictures belong to their respective owners. Wizzair Virtual is not affiliated with Wizz Air, Infinite Flight, or any other organization.
If you would like to visit Wizz Air's official website, please click here.

WHY SHOULD YOU JOIN ?

A virtual airline where all is possible

  • Have you ever been interested in exploring the unknown parts of Europe, and what's more, eventually get perks for it?
  • Have you ever wanted to try a fabulous realistic career mode program where you could fly the exact flights that Wizzair does in real life at the exact time they do it?
  • Have you ever thought about doing awesome group flights across many countries with other amazing pilots all this whilst wearing the beautiful Wizzair colors?

Our staff is constantly thinking of creating interesting and fun events or activities to perform for the VA so if your answers to the above questions were positive, do not hesitate a second to join us for an amazing adventure.
